Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how {{ company }} collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, including:

  • Contact form submissions: name, email address, and message content.
  • WhatsApp enquiries: information shared through WhatsApp is governed by WhatsApp's own privacy policy.
  • Order information: name, email, phone number, and billing address when you request an invoice.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to enquiries and provide customer support.
  • To process orders and generate invoices.
  • To send transactional communications related to your order.
  • To improve our website and services.

3. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes for which it was collected, or as required by law. Contact form data is retained for 24 months. Order records are retained for 7 years for accounting compliance.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data to third parties. We may share data with:

  • Service providers who assist us in operating our website (e.g., hosting providers).
  • Logistics partners when fulfilling chemical shipments (limited to delivery details).
  • Law enforcement or regulatory authorities when required by law.

5. Cookies

Our website uses only essential session cookies required for the site to function. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies. No third-party analytics scripts are loaded without your consent.

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Object to or restrict processing of your data.
  • Data portability (receive your data in a structured format).

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at {{ email }}.

7. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including HTTPS encryption, secure session management, and access controls. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes by posting a notice on our website. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance.
