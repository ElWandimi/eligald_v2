Company Profile

Built on a Foundation of Scientific Integrity

Founded in 1994, Eligald Industrial Chemicals Limited has grown from a regional distributor into a globally connected chemical supply partner. Headquartered in Industrial City, we operate blending facilities, a certified QC laboratory, and a network of logistics partners spanning over 30 countries.

We supply manufacturers, pharmaceutical companies, research institutions, water-treatment authorities, and agri-processors. Every product we handle — from commodity acids to rare specialty reagents — is sourced from audited manufacturers and re-verified in our own laboratory before dispatch.

Our team of 85 professionals includes registered chemists, supply-chain specialists, and certified hazardous-goods transport managers. That combination of scientific rigour and operational excellence is what makes Eligald a trusted name in the industry.
